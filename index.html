Welcome to The Green House Bed and Breakfast, in operation for the past 20 years ideally located in Galway Bay. Our bed and breakfast offers a comfortable setting while in Galway. It is ideally located for those wishing to discover the area's attractions.

This 4-star hotel offers a 24-hour reception, wireless internet and luggage storage. The stylish rooms at The Green House offers a private bathroom and all the essentials for a comfortable stay. Each provide ironing facilities, wireless internet access and tea and coffee making facilities. The Green House Bed and Breakfastboth a restaurant and a bar where guests are able to relax of an evening with a meal and a drink.

We Offer:

  • Luxuriously furnished rooms with walnut wooden floors & central heating
  • Own private bathrooms with full size baths & walk-in tropical rain showers
  • Free 24 hour wireless internet connection & 32" to 40" flat T.V.
  • Full breakfast & Tea and Coffee making facilities & Guest fridge freezer
  • Iron and ironing board & hair dryer & Free private off street car park
  • We are a NO smoking establishment

The Green House provides a convenient place to discover Galway Bay and Eyre Square. Under a 20-minute drive away, Galway Airport is within easy reach.
