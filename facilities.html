Guest Facilities

Eyre Square Shopping Centre: Located in the heart of Galway has been described as Irelands most modern and yet historic shopping centre. The centre is Galway City's liveliest and attractive meeting place incorporating over 60 national and international shopping outlets. read more...

Restaurants and Traditional Irish Music Pubs: Pubs in ireland were traditionally not the place to go for a full meal but now compete with restaurants for the quality of their food. The standard and type of pub food varies enormously but here is a small guide to some of the best. read more...

Sandy Beaches: The beach at Salthill is located in Galway city and overlooking Galway Bay. The beach is lifeguarded from mid-May to the end of September from 11 am to 7 pm. Salthill beach has been a blue flag beach and enormously popular in fine weather during the summer. read more...

Spectacular Coastal Walks: There are many highly recommended walks, for instance the Ardmore Cliff Walk (5km, 60 mins), Cunnigar - a long sandy strip across the mouth of Dungarvan Bay(6km, 90 mins), and Lady Louisaâ€™s Walk from Lismore Castle (2 km, 30 mins) read more...

Horse-riding, Golfing: Horseriding takes in the beaches, back-country trails and mountains on short rides and treks. The Green House B and B is well placed for golfing holidays in Ireland.

Fishing: There is rock fishing at Ardmore Head where wrasse, conger, pollock, codling, coalfish and mackerel are all common. Please ask reception for complimentary fishing equipment.
